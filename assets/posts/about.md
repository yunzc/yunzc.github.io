Hi, my name is Yun Chang and I am a graduate student in SPARK (Sensing, Perception, and Robot Kinetics) Lab at MIT. I work on multirobot robust SLAM and I am involed in the [DARPA Subterranean Challenge](https://www.subtchallenge.com/) with team [CoSTAR](https://costar.jpl.nasa.gov/). In my free time I enjoy playing the piano, reading, and going on hikes. Hoping to be able to share some of the research I am involved in, some projects I am working on, and perhaps also some writings. 

### Education

- M.S. in Aeronautics and Astronautics. Massachusetts Institute of Technology (2019 ~ 2020)
