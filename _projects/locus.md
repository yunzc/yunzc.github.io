LOCUS (Lidar Odometry for Consistent operation in Uncertain Settings) is the lidar odometry solution we deployed on various platforms like the Clearpath Husky and the Boston Dynamic Spot during the
[DARPA Subterranean Challenge](https://www.darpa.mil/program/darpa-subterranean-challenge).
It is a multi-sensor lidar-centric solution for prevision odometry and 3D mapping in real-time.
We open sourced the code along with a few datasets taken from tunnels and caves we tested in when preparing for the challenge.

Please check out our [repo](https://github.com/NeBula-Autonomy/LOCUS).
